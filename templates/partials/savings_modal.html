The "Pay Yourself First" Method

TrackMyRupee is designed around a simple but powerful rule: Treat Savings as an Expense.

Income Arrives

Your monthly salary hits your account.

Savings are Deducted

Record SIPs, RDs, and Investments as an Expense Category named "Savings".

True Balance

Income - Expenses (incl. Savings) = Your Guilt-Free Spending Money.
