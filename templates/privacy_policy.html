1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, input financial data, or interact with our platform.

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and password (encrypted).
  • Financial Data: Incomes, expenses, categories, budgets, and subscriptions you manually enter.
  • Profile Preferences: Currency settings, theme preferences (light/dark mode).
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, browser type, and interaction logs to help us improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services.

  • Provide the TrackMyRupee dashboard, expense tracking, and financial reports.
  • Process and visualize your financial data.
  • Send important notifications, such as password resets or account alerts.
  • Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze the use of our Service. Google Analytics involves the use of cookies to collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our platform.

You can opt-out of having your activity available to Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors (e.g., hosting providers, email services, analytics) who assist our operations.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, or legal process to protect the rights, property, or safety of TrackMyRupee or others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

  • Encryption of sensitive data (like passwords) at rest.
  • Secure HTTPS (TLS/SSL) for all data in transit.
  • Regular backups and security assessments.
  • Access controls to limit internal access to personal data.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your personal information.

  • Access & Correction: You can view and edit your profile and financial data directly within the application.
  • Data Export: You can export your data (e.g., to CSV/Excel) at any time.
  • Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account and all associated data permanently.
  • Communications: You can manage your email preferences or unsubscribe from non-essential emails.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account remains active.
  • Deleted Accounts: If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our live databases immediately and from backups within 30-90 days.

8. Children's Privacy

TrackMyRupee is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page or sending you an email. Your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at:
support@trackmyrupee.com
